Bland Chicken

Where I started

While I love cooking now, it wasn't always this way. I used to dread cooking, mostly because I didn't understand what I was doing or have a clear idea of the sorts of flavors that I enjoy.

Look at it! It's Rawwww!!!

Oddly enough, I grew up watching Hell's Kitchen with my parents. I'd watch Gordon Ramsay swear up a storm while telling a contestant what a poor job they did cooking something. Over time, this led me to have a fear of undercooking food for a while.

Cook it through

While I've moved past the fear of undercooking food, I figured it'd be fun to reflect on how I used to cook chicken as a child. Strap in this gets complex...

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • A pot of boiling water.
  • Some salt (not too much because I was afraid of having too much sodium) and granulated garlic for fLaVoR

Preperation

  1. Boil water
  2. Put in chicken breasts carefully (Hey at least I was careful!).
  3. Add seasoning and stir
  4. Set a timer for 30 minutes.
  5. Drain the water.
  6. "Enjoy" your chicken
